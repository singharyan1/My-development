The Confluence & Harihar Nath

Sonepur is famous for the convergence of the Gandaki and Ganges rivers, creating a deeply sacred environment. Pilgrims visit the Harihar Nath temple to offer their respects to the intertwined deities of Vishnu and Shiva.
